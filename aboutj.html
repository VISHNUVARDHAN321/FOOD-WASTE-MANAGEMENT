HUNGRY DEATHS

Hungry deaths, also known as deaths due to starvation or malnutrition, occur when individuals do not have access to an adequate and nutritious food supply for an extended period, leading to severe health consequences and, in extreme cases, death. Hunger is a complex and multifaceted issue influenced by various factors, including poverty, lack of access to food, conflict, environmental conditions, and political instability.
